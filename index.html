About

Welcome to my page! Take a minute to look around. If you find that we share common objectives in the world of data, feel free to reach out! I'd love to hear from you. I have a year of experience being a Data Scientist, and three years as a Python developer. Most of my work so far is concentrated around text analysis, neural networks, and image analysis, though I have dabbled a bit in regression modeling. I am thoroughly enjoying my work with industrial data, but personally my interests are centered around using data to address civic issues that directly impact the day-to-day lives of members of my community. For example, this includes the healthcare system, socio-economic concerns, and the impact of external factors on students' academic performance. In today's world, data is abundant and easily accessible. It is only a matter of putting together the right pieces of information to unlease the power within.

In my development environment, you will find: Python, R, Anaconda, Jupyter, Tensorflow
